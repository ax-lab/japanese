# で

## ① Copula

Connective form of the copula [だ](だ). See also [～て](＿て).

## ② Contextual particle

Specifies the context in which an action is performed (e.g. "by way of").

Note the use with 何:

> 何できた？　　　なにできた？　　Came by way of what?
> バスで来た　　　　　　　　　　　Came by bus.
> 
> 何できた？　　　なんできた？　　Why did you come?
> 暇だから　　　　　　　　　　　　Because I'm free.
